# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** May 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Jaskirat Chawla ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use our automation services, including WhatsApp-based lead management and CRM workflows (the "Services").

## 1. Information We Collect

When you interact with the Services, we may collect and process:

- **Contact information** (such as name, phone number, WhatsApp ID)
- **Message content** you send to the bot or service
- **Lead and CRM data** (such as residence, location preference, budget, follow-up notes, assignment details, and meeting dates)
- **Technical metadata** (such as message timestamps, delivery status, workflow execution logs, and error logs)

## 2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide and operate the Services
- Create, update, and manage CRM records
- Route and assign leads to team members
- Send operational notifications (for example, assignee alerts)
- Monitor, troubleshoot, secure, and improve workflow reliability
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. Third-Party Processors and Platforms

To deliver the Services, data may be processed by third-party providers, including:

- **Meta / WhatsApp Business Platform**
- **OpenAI**
- **n8n (workflow automation platform)**
- **Google (for Google Sheets and related services)**

Each provider processes data under its own terms and privacy policies.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell personal information.

We may share information only:

- With the service providers listed above to run the Services
- With authorized team members for CRM operations
- If required by law, regulation, legal process, or valid government request
- To protect rights, security, and integrity of the Services

## 5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide and operate the Services, maintain business records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations.

Data may be deleted or anonymized when no longer required for these purposes.

## 6. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information. However, no internet-based system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information.

To make a request, contact us using the details below.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Services are not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Because our service providers may operate in multiple countries, your information may be processed outside your country of residence.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
