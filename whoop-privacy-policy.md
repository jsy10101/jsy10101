# WHOOP Integration Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

This privacy policy describes how this personal WHOOP integration handles data accessed through the WHOOP API.

## Purpose

This app is currently used for personal development and testing. It may request access to WHOOP data only so the account owner can view, analyze, or experiment with their own fitness, recovery, sleep, workout, cycle, profile, and body measurement data.

## Data Collected

Depending on the permissions granted in the WHOOP OAuth flow, the app may access the following WHOOP data:

- Profile information
- Recovery information, including recovery score, heart rate variability, and resting heart rate
- Cycle information, including strain and heart rate data
- Sleep information, including sleep duration and start/end times
- Workout information, including activity type and strain
- Body measurement information, including height, weight, and max heart rate

## How Data Is Used

Data accessed through WHOOP is used only for personal testing, development, and analytics. The data is not sold, rented, or shared with advertisers.

## Data Storage

During development, data may be temporarily processed or stored locally or in development systems controlled by the app owner. Reasonable care will be taken to avoid exposing sensitive information.

## Data Sharing

This app does not intentionally share WHOOP data with third parties, except where required to operate development infrastructure or comply with applicable law.

## Data Deletion

Users can revoke access through their WHOOP account or OAuth permissions. If you want data associated with this app deleted, contact the app owner using the contact information listed in the WHOOP developer app settings.

## Security

Reasonable technical safeguards are used during development, but this app is not yet a production commercial service.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the app owner using the contact email listed in the WHOOP developer dashboard.
